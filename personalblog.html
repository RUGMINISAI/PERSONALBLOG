ABOUT ME

A small world....

I am Rugmini Sai, if I have to define myself I would say I am a bit of a trouble maker,nature lover, chatterbox, a friend and an unpaid therapist to my friends. My earliest of memories was when I was around 5 years old, I remember clutching on a white sock, maybe that is my first every registered memory. Whether it is first or second over the years I have made lots of memories with different feelings, some good, enjoyable, sad, heart wrenching and so on. As everyone says your school life is a rose-tinted one, beautiful memories and long lasting friends but that doesn't seems to be me, my rose-tinted life started in college, why you say, well because I am in a women's college!
Let's take a dive into my life!!!
